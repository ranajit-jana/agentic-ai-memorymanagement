ESCALATION CASE #ESC-0101
Date Opened: 2024-01-08 | Priority: Critical | Status: Resolved
Customer: Vikram Choudhary | Product: Apple iPhone 15 Pro | Order: YNC-480021
Reason for Escalation: Customer claims delivered phone has IMEI mismatch with invoice.
================================================================================
SUMMARY:
Customer received the iPhone 15 Pro and noticed the IMEI on the device does not match the IMEI printed on the invoice and box. Customer is concerned about receiving a stolen or tampered device. Has threatened to file a police complaint.

ACTION TAKEN:
- Immediately escalated to Quality & Authenticity Team (QAT) on Jan 8.
- QAT confirmed: IMEI mismatch due to a documentation error at the supplier end. Device is genuine.
- Issued a corrected invoice with correct IMEI on Jan 10.
- Offered Rs 1000 credit + extended warranty for inconvenience.
- Customer accepted resolution. No police complaint filed.

RESOLVED: 2024-01-10 | Resolution Time: 2 days
ROOT CAUSE: Supplier packing documentation error — escalated to vendor management.

ESCALATION CASE #ESC-0145
Date Opened: 2024-02-05 | Priority: Critical | Status: Under Review
Customer: Suresh Natarajan | Product: Multiple orders (6 items) | Order IDs: YNC-501xxx series
Reason for Escalation: Customer alleges account was hacked, orders placed without consent. Disputes Rs 1.2 lakh in charges.
================================================================================
SUMMARY:
Customer reported that 6 high-value orders were placed from their account on Feb 3rd. Customer was travelling and did not place these orders. All orders shipped to a different delivery address. Customer's credit card was also charged. Total disputed amount: Rs 1,19,450.

ACTION TAKEN:
- Account immediately suspended pending investigation on Feb 5.
- All 6 orders placed on hold (3 already shipped, 3 in warehouse).
- Cybersecurity & Fraud team notified.
- Coordinating with logistics to intercept the 3 shipped orders.
- Payment team reviewing chargeback process with customer's bank.
- Customer advised to file a complaint with cyber crime cell.

STATUS AS OF 2024-03-01: 2 of 3 shipped orders intercepted. 1 delivered to fraud address — police complaint filed by YNC. Refund for 5 orders processed. 1 order refund pending bank chargeback confirmation.
ROOT CAUSE: Account password compromise via phishing link. Security team sent advisory email to all customers.

ESCALATION CASE #ESC-0162
Date Opened: 2024-02-14 | Priority: High | Status: Resolved
Customer: Anita Sharma | Product: Dyson Airwrap | Order: YNC-506712
Reason for Escalation: Customer received empty box. Alleges item was stolen in transit.
================================================================================
SUMMARY:
Customer opened the Dyson Airwrap delivery box to find it empty except for some crumpled paper. The outer box was sealed with YNC tape and showed no signs of tampering externally. Customer took unboxing video. Highly distressed. Product cost: Rs 37,900.

ACTION TAKEN:
- Reviewed customer's unboxing video — clearly shows empty box.
- Checked warehouse dispatch CCTV footage for order YNC-506712. Footage confirmed product was packed and sealed correctly at warehouse.
- Evidence points to tampering during transit by third-party logistics.
- Replacement unit dispatched immediately on Feb 15 without waiting for investigation to close.
- FIR filed by YNC's legal team against the logistics partner.
- Customer received replacement on Feb 17.

RESOLVED: 2024-02-17 | Resolution Time: 3 days
ROOT CAUSE: Transit theft by logistics personnel. Partner contract reviewed.
